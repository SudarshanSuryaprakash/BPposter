Mission

To save the time and energy of both customers and store clerks by providing useful information about a selected product on demand

Vision

To revelutionize in-store customer experience at clothing stores world-wide

MODE

Mode is a software that allows customers to easily obtain useful information about a clothing product right on their smartphone.

Available sizes and colors of a scanned product are displayed for the ease of access of the customer!
